This is the recipe for my
version of Vegan Opor .

Ingredients:
Thai/Indonesian shallots 200grams
Garlic 3 cloves
Galanggal 2 roots
Lemongrass 2 sticks
Chili 2 chillies
Candle nuts 10 nuts
Ginger 1 thumb


All ingredients above are to blend to paste

Tempe 1 pack cut to 1,5 cm cube
Tofu 1 pack cut to 1,5 cm cube
Eggplant 1, Slice the eggplant 0,5 cm thickness round shape
Dried Tofu Skin 1/2 pack, break it to 5 cm long and soak it in water for 2 hours until soft
Coconut milk 1,25 litre
Salam/bay leaves 2 leaves
Lime leaves 3 leaves
Water use accordingly

All ingredients are available here


Instructions:

  1. Heat sunflower seed oil in a pot.
  2. Put the "bumbu" paste in and heat it until it release the aroma.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and put in the leaves.
  4. Cook until it boils and add salt and pepper until you reached the preferred saltiness.
  5. When it boils, put in the veggies, tofu and tempe.
  6. Add some water to keep the consistency of the sauce a bit light. It shouldnâ€™t be a thick sauce.
  7. Cook in boiling coconut water until the eggplant is really soft.
  8. Serve with jasmine rice and top it up with fried onions.


enjoy!
